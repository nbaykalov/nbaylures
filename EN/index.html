About Me ...

Hello, my name is Nikola. As a man grew up in the most beautiful mountain in Bulgaria, namely the Rhodope Mountains, I practice some hobbies related to the mountain. The first of these is fishing. I started at 6-7 years of age with the fishing of small trout with 5 meters of cord and a hook, rolled up by a willow branch. Consequently, over the years, enthusiasm grew. I tried fishing for other kind of fish with different rods and techniques. As the years go by, leisure has been decreasing, it has already been fully engaged, and fishing has been getting less and less time. I had to choose which type of fishing I like the most and practice only it - this was the trout fishing. At one point I decided to start making some of my baits myself. The experience not only was successful, but at the time I was catching almost entirely the lures I made myself. Here I have presented a great deal of them.
