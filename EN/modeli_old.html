Handmade lures

Wobblers are made of balsa, PVC, cork and other materials suitable for the purpose. The blade is made of extremely sturdy epoxy fiberglass, which reduces the risk of unpleasant breaking of the blade during fishing. Use Paint on Createx, but to impregnate the wobble before paint - propionate. A luretane lacquer, applied in several layers, is used as the final coating. The end result is an extremely good wobbler, which will give you a lot of emotions during fishing.
