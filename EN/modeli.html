Models of NBay Lures - Feel The Differnet

Wobblers are made of balsa, PVC, cork and other materials suitable for the purpose. The blade is made of extremely sturdy epoxy fiberglass, which reduces the risk of unpleasant breaking of the blade during fishing. Use Paint on Createx, but to impregnate the wobble before paint - propionate. A luretane lacquer, applied in several layers, is used as the final coating. The result is an extremely good wobbler, which will give you a lot of emotions during fishing.

The 30s is a sinking wobbler specially designed to awaken even the most passive trout. It has been successfully tested in the rivers of the Rhodopes and the Balkan Mountains. Suitable for small rivers and streams.

↪ Length: 30mm
↪Weight: 0.8-1.4g
↪Sinking
